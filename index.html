Suicide Rate around the world

From looking at the map below, one can assume that the stronger a country's economy is, the more likely people will suicide. However, There are many outlier that put the statement into question.

It can be seen that although the map shows a relationship between economy and suicide rate, The bubble chart shows otherwise with most of countries having similar suicide rate and umeployment rate. The GDP of countries also seems to not affect the suicide rate as 2 nations with similar suicide rate can have 2 vastly different GDP.
